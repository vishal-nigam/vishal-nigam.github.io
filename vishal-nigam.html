About

Why Investilo AI?

As an investor who has spent years working with AI and statistics, I kept running into the same wall: to answer one serious question about a stock, you end up clicking through filings, earnings calls, news, price charts, macro reports, and old notes across different tools. By the time you've pulled everything together, you're tired—and still worried you've missed something.

Investilo AI grew out of that experience. I started by asking: "What if the heavy lifting could be done by a system that reads like an analyst, not just scrapes numbers?" Today, my team and I are building AI that pulls key information into one place, highlights what has actually changed, shows the trail back to original sources, and helps people move faster from raw data to a clear, considered view of the business.

Applied AI in Practice

Across 16+ years in AI, data analytics, and product-led delivery, I've built and led teams working on practical ML adoption in industry. My work focuses on the boundary between automation and augmentation—how to keep humans meaningfully in the loop, when to trust outputs, and how to engineer safeguards so systems remain dependable as data and context evolve.
